POTENTIAL IMPACT
-----------------

* A TSUNAMI IS A SERIES OF WAVES. TIME BETWEEN WAVE PEAKS
    CAN VARY FROM 5 MINUTES TO ONE HOUR. HAZARDS THAT MAY EXIST
    FOR HOURS OR LONGER AFTER THE FIRST WAVE.

* IMPACTS CAN VARY DRAMATICALLY FROM A PART OF THE COAST TO
    NEXT DUE TO LOCAL DEPTH MEASUREMENT AND SHAPE AND ALTITUDE
    OF THE COAST.

*THE EFFECTS MAY ALSO VARY DEPENDING ON THE STATE OF THE TIDE AT
    THE DURATION OF THE TSUNAMI IS MAXIMUM.

* PEOPLE TRAPPED IN TSUNAMI WATER COULD DROWN... BE
    CRUSHED BY DEBRIS IN THE WATER... OR BE SWEPT INTO THE SEA.
