Flood Statement
New Orleans LA National Weather Service
731 PM CDT Saturday, May 3, 2025

... Continued flood warnings for the following rivers in 
Louisiana...

Mississippi River At Sanctuary affects St. John the Baptist, St. 
  Charles and St. James Parish.

Mississippi River At Donaldsonville affects Ascension Parish.

Mississippi River at Baton Rouge affects West Baton Rouge and 
  East Baton Rouge Parish.

Mississippi River At Red River Landing affects Pointe Coupee, 
  West Feliciana and East Baton Rouge parishes.

For the lower Mississippi River... including Red River Landing, Baton 
Rouge, Donaldsonville, Reserve... Major flooding is forecast.

PREVENTIVE/PREPARATORY ACTIONS...

Use caution when walking near the riverbank.

Drivers should not attempt to drive around obstacles or drive 
cars through flooded areas.

Additional information is available at www.weather.gov/lix.  Click 
on the Rivers and Lakes menu for forecasting and observation.

The next statement will be made on Sunday morning at 1145 AM CDT.
