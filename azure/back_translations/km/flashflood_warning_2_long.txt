Flood Statement
New Orleans LA National Weather Service
731 PM CDT Saturday, May 3, 2025

... Flood warnings continue for the following rivers in 
Louisiana...

The Mississippi River at the Reserve affects St. John the Baptist, St. 
  Charles and St. James Community Church.

The Mississippi River in Donaldsonville affects Ascension Parish.

The Mississippi River in Baton Rouge affects West Baton Rouge and 
  East Baton Rouge Community Church.

Mississippi River at Red River Landing affects Pointe Coupee, 
  West Feliciana and East Baton Rouge parishes.

Rouge, Donaldsonville, Reserve... Major flooding is predicted.

Action Caution/Readiness...

Caution is urged when walking near the river banks.

Drivers should not attempt to drive around obstacles or drive 
Vehicles passing through flooded areas.

on the Rivers and Lakes menu for forecasts and observations.
