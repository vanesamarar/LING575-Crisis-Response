If you find yourself in this coastal area, move inland 
to higher ground.

Tsunami warnings mean that a tsunami with
Flooding is possible or is already occurring.  Tsunamis are
a series of dangerous waves many hours after the initial arrival
Hour.  The first wave may not be the largest.

Preliminary magnitude 7.5 occurred 35 miles southeast of

Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, 550 PM.  EDT. July 8.
