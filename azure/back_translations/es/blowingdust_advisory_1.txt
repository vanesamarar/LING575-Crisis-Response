  Hudspeth Counties and western El Paso County.

*IMPACTS... Dangerous driving conditions due to reduced visibility.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS... Showers and weak storms over southern New York 
  Mexico is producing 30 to 40 mph departure winds, which are 
  pushing east toward the I-10 corridor between Las Cruces and 
  Downtown El Paso. These outflow limits are collecting dust and 
  pushing it east into the Rio Grande Valley.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

People with respiratory problems should make preparations to stay 
indoors until the storm passes. Prepare for a sudden drop 
Almost zero visibility. If you encounter dust blowing or blowing 
sand on the road or see it approaching, move away from the road as far away as possible 
as possible and park your vehicle. Turn on the lights all the time 
and keep your foot off the brake pedal. Remember, 'Step aside, 
Stay alive.'
