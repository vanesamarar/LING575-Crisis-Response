POTENTIAL IMPACTS
-----------------

* A TSUNAMI IS A SERIES OF WAVES. THE TIME BETWEEN THE CRESTS OF THE WAVES
    IT CAN RANGE FROM 5 MINUTES TO AN HOUR. DANGER CAN PERSIST
    FOR MANY HOURS OR MORE AFTER THE INITIAL WAVE.

*IMPACTS CAN VARY SIGNIFICANTLY FROM ONE SECTION OF COASTLINE TO ANOTHER.
    THE NEXT DUE TO THE LOCAL BATHYMETRY AND THE SHAPE AND ELEVATION
    OF THE COAST.

* IMPACTS MAY ALSO VARY DEPENDING ON THE TIDE STATE IN
    THE TIMING OF THE TSUNAMI'S PEAK WAVES.

* PEOPLE CAUGHT IN TSUNAMI WATER CAN DROWN... BE
    CRUSHED BY DEBRIS IN THE WATER... OR BE SWEPT OUT TO SEA.
