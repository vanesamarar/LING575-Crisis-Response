IMPACT
-------
The impact will vary at different locations within the recommendation.
area.

If you are in an area with a tsunami warning;

 * Tsunami is possible with strong waves and currents.

 * Waves and currents can drown or injure people.
   domestic.

 * Currents at beaches and in harbors, marinas,
   Bays and estuaries can be particularly dangerous.

 * Some effects may last for hours to days after
   the appearance of the first wave.

 * The first wave may not be the biggest so the following waves may
   become bigger.

 * Each wave can last from 5 to 45 minutes as a wave comes in.
   and retreat.

 * Coasts in all directions are threatened because
   Waves can lap around islands, headlands and spill into bays.

 * Strong shaking or rolling of the ground indicates a
   An earthquake has occurred and a tsunami may be imminent.

 * Retreating or rapidly receding shorelines, erratic waves and
   Strong sounds and currents are signs of a tsunami.

 * Tsunamis can occur when water moves rapidly out to sea,
   a tide that rises gently like a flood without breaking waves,
   like a series of breaking waves, or a wall of foam.
