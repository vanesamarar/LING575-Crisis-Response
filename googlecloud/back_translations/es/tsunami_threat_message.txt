ASSESSMENT

    FORECAST FOR SOME COASTS.


TSUNAMI THREAT FORECAST...UPDATED
---------------------------------

  * TSUNAMI WAVES THAT REACH 1 TO 3 METERS ABOVE THE TIDE LEVEL ARE
    POSSIBLE ALONG SOME COASTS OF

      CHILI.


  * TSUNAMI WAVES REACHING 0.3 TO 1 METERS ABOVE TIDE LEVEL
    THEY ARE POSSIBLE FOR SOME COASTS OF

      ANTARCTICA.


  * ACTUAL AMPLITUDES ON THE COAST MAY VARY FROM THE FORECAST
    AMPLITUDES DUE TO FORECAST AND LOCAL UNCERTAINTIES
    CHARACTERISTICS. IN PARTICULAR, MAXIMUM TSUNAMI AMPLITUDES ON ATOLLS
    AND IN PLACES WITH REEFS OR BARRIER REEFS PROBABLY
    IT WILL BE MUCH SMALLER THAN THE FORECAST INDICATES.

  * FOR OTHER AREAS COVERED BY THIS PRODUCT NO FORECAST HAS BEEN MADE
    NOT YET CALCULATED. FORECAST WILL BE EXTENDED IF
    REQUIRED FOR LATER PRODUCTS.


RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
-------------------

  * GOVERNMENT AGENCIES RESPONSIBLE FOR THREATENED COASTAL AREAS
    MUST TAKE STEPS TO INFORM AND INSTRUCT ANY COASTAL PERSON
    POPULATIONS AT RISK ACCORDING TO THEIR OWN
    EVALUATION...PROCEDURES AND THREAT LEVEL.

  * PEOPLE IN THREATENED COASTAL AREAS SHOULD REMAIN ALERT
    FOR INFORMATION AND TO FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS OF THE NATIONAL AUTHORITIES AND
    LOCAL AUTHORITIES.

POTENTIAL IMPACTS
-----------------

  * A TSUNAMI IS A SERIES OF WAVES. THE TIME BETWEEN THE WAVE CRESTS
    MAY VARY FROM 5 MINUTES TO ONE HOUR. DANGER MAY PERSIST.
    FOR MANY HOURS OR MORE AFTER THE INITIAL WAVE.

  * IMPACTS CAN VARY SIGNIFICANTLY FROM ONE SECTION OF COAST TO ANOTHER.
    THE FOLLOWING DUE TO LOCAL BATHYMETRY AND SHAPE AND ELEVATION
    FROM THE COAST.

  * IMPACTS MAY ALSO VARY DEPENDING ON THE STATE OF THE TIDE IN
    THE TIME OF MAXIMUM TSUNAMI WAVES.

  * PEOPLE TRAPPED IN TSUNAMI WATERS CAN DROWN...
    CRUSHED BY DEBRIS IN THE WATER... OR WIPED OUT TO SEA.
