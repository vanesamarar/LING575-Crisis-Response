AVALANCHE WARNING IN RURAL AREAS IN EFFECT FROM 6 AM MST

  Wasatch Range, Uinta Mountains, Wasatch Plateau, Manti Horizon, 
  The Abajos and the Tushar Range. 

* WHEN...Effective from 6 am MST this morning until 6 am MST Monday. 

* IMPACTS...Heavy snowfall and drifts caused by strong winds have created
  Large areas of unstable snow and very dangerous avalanches
  Conditions at all altitudes. Natural and man-made.
  Avalanches are certain. Travel is recommended to avoid in all areas.
  avalanche terrain and stay away from avalanche slide zones. Avoid being
  on or under terrain with a slope greater than 30 degrees.
