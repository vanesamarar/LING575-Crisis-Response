...WINTER WEATHER ADVISORY IN EFFECT FROM 6 AM SUNDAY TO 6 AM MDT 
TUESDAY ABOVE 9000FT...

* WHAT...Snow expected above 9000ft. Total snow accumulations 
  between 5 and 15 inches.

* WHERE...Southern Mountains, primarily elevations above 9000ft.

* WHEN...From 6 AM Sunday to 6 AM MDT Tuesday.

* IMPACTS...Travel could be very difficult along SR-12 for 
  elevations above 9,000ft during the overnight hours as 
  temperatures become cold enough for accumulating snowfall. 
  Significant impacts to travel are not anticipated during the 
  daylight hours.

Any outdoor recreationalists should expect periods of moderate to 
heavy snowfall in the high elevations (above 9,000ft MSL) on Sunday 
and Monday afternoon.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...
